# PitchIQ Brand Architecture

## Brand Hierarchy

### Parent Company
**WellTrack Performance** is the parent company and commercial umbrella for the product family.

### Technology Platform
**WellTrackIQ** is the technology platform layer that powers the performance-intelligence product suite.

### Current Product
**PitchIQ** is the flagship soccer performance product under WellTrack Performance and WellTrackIQ.

PitchIQ remains the product display name. The repository, package names, runtime routes, and application architecture should not be renamed as part of this brand architecture.

## Product Family Naming Convention

Future products should follow the IQ naming system where appropriate:

- **CoachIQ** — coach-facing intelligence and planning tools
- **ScanIQ** — scanning and perception training
- **TeamIQ** — team-level performance intelligence
- **MindIQ** — mindset, regulation, and psychological skills
- **RehabIQ** — rehabilitation and return-to-play support

## Governance Rule

All future products should sit under the **WellTrack Performance / WellTrackIQ** architecture unless explicitly changed by executive decision.

## Usage Rule

- Use **PitchIQ** as the user-facing product name.
- Use **WellTrackIQ** when referring to the underlying platform.
- Use **WellTrack Performance** when referring to the parent company, commercial umbrella, or product family.
