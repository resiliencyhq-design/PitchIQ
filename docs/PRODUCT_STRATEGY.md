# PitchIQ Product Strategy Bible v1.0

Vision: Train the brain behind the game.

PitchIQ is a football cognition training platform for youth players, combining short daily missions, camera/voice reaction training, XP, rewards, player identity and progression.

Core loop:
Open app → Daily mission → Training → Camera challenge → XP → Reward → Progress → Return tomorrow.

Metrics are training indicators, not diagnostic or fixed-ability measures.
