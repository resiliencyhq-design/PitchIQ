PROJECT: PitchIQ

MISSION:
Build the best cognitive football training app for youth players.

PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY:
PitchIQ is designed as an Academy experience rather than a traditional educational application.

The player should never feel like they are completing forms or assessments.

Instead, the player should feel that they are:
- Entering an academy.
- Creating their football identity.
- Completing an academy trial.
- Being evaluated.
- Earning selection.
- Signing an academy contract.
- Receiving their academy kit.
- Beginning their football intelligence journey.

Every design decision should strengthen this narrative.

LOCKED MVP BLUEPRINT:
The PitchIQ MVP is the complete Academy Journey described in docs/MVP_BLUEPRINT.md.

1. Landing Screen
2. Player Identity
3. Choose Position
4. Academy Trial
5. Scout Report
6. Sign Your Contract
7. Contract Signed
8. Your Academy Player
9. Academy Kit
10. Home Screen

RULES:
- MVP first
- Academy Journey first
- Prototype before infrastructure
- No feature creep
- One objective per sprint
- Do not redesign without approval
- Do not add disconnected features before the MVP Blueprint is complete

CURRENT PRIORITY:
Complete and polish the Academy Journey blueprint stages before Beta expansion.
