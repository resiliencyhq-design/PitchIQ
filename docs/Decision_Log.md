# PitchIQ Decision Log

## 001 Freeze MVP
The MVP is locked to six screens: Splash, Create Academy Player, Home, Training, Results, Player.

## 002 Create Product Bible
The Product Bible becomes the governing product/design/engineering reference.

## 003 Establish PitchIQ HQ
PitchIQ HQ becomes the starting point for every development session.

## 004 Separate HQ and App
PitchIQ HQ manages the product. PitchIQ App trains the player.

## 005 Defer Version 2 Features
Career, Shop, Analytics, Apple Watch, LiDAR, IMU, AI Coach, Marketplace, and Social Features are deferred unless approved.
