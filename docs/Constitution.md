# PitchIQ Constitution

## Purpose
PitchIQ is a youth football cognitive performance product. It exists to help players train faster, think faster, and become better footballers.

## Separation of Systems
PitchIQ HQ manages the product. PitchIQ App trains the player. These systems must remain separate.

## MVP Governance
The MVP is locked to six screens:

1. Splash
2. Create Academy Player
3. Home
4. Training
5. Results
6. Player

No additional features may enter MVP without Executive approval.

## Deferred to Version 2
Career, Shop, Analytics, Apple Watch, LiDAR, IMU, AI Coach, Marketplace, and Social Features are deferred unless explicitly approved.

## Engineering Constraints
Do not modify routing, XP, rewards, scoring, training engine, storage schema, app shell, or bottom navigation unless explicitly approved.

## Product Rule
Consistency beats novelty. Every sprint must protect the MVP and reduce product risk.
