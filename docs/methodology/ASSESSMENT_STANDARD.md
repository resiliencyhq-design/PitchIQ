# PitchIQ Assessment Design Standard v1.0

**Status:** Locked

## Construct purity

Each Academy assessment measures and develops one unmistakable primary Football IQ construct. Supporting constructs may contribute but must not overshadow the primary construct.

Assessment is for precision. Training may integrate multiple constructs.

## Required specification sections

Every assessment must define:

1. Purpose and primary construct
2. Scientific foundation
3. Representative football context
4. Player experience and emotional objective
5. Gameplay loop
6. Progressive difficulty
7. Scoring model
8. Match Mentality capture
9. Coaching feedback
10. Training integration
11. Expected football transfer
12. Validation questions
13. Success criterion

## Design tests

Every assessment must:

- represent football rather than a generic brain game;
- work reliably on a phone;
- be repeatable and sensitive to improvement;
- remain age-appropriate;
- produce actionable feedback;
- measure response time without allowing speed to outweigh decision quality;
- avoid trick questions and artificial gotcha moments;
- avoid overstating transfer until supported by validation.

## Golden question

> If this score improves, what football behaviour should improve on the pitch?

If that question cannot be answered clearly, the task is not ready.

## North Star

> Feel like football. Teach like a coach. Measure like science. Inspire like a mentor.
