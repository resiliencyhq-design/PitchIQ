# Assessment 2 — Read the Play

**Codename:** RTP-01  
**Status:** Locked

**Primary FIQ:** Game Reading  
**Secondary FIQ:** Awareness, Decision Quality

## Purpose

Measure recognition of emerging football patterns and anticipation of what is likely to happen next.

## Core question

> What do those clues mean?

## Gameplay

1. Watch a short football sequence.
2. Play freezes before a meaningful event.
3. Predict the next pass, movement, danger or area of development.
4. Resume the sequence to reveal the outcome.
5. Receive brief coaching feedback.

## Measures

- pattern recognition;
- anticipation accuracy;
- tactical cue use;
- decision readiness;
- response time, secondary to quality.

## Match Mentality

Persistence, recovery, learning, composure and consistency across increasingly complex scenarios.

## Transfer target

Earlier anticipation, improved defensive positioning, smarter pressing, better attacking support and off-ball movement.

## Coaching message

> Recognise the clues. Predict the play.

## Success criterion

The player leaves understanding that football gives clues before the next action occurs.
