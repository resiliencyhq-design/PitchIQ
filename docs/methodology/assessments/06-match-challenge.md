# Academy Final — Match Challenge

**Codename:** MC-01  
**Status:** Locked

**Purpose:** Integrate and validate the five Football IQ constructs in representative match-like scenarios. Match Challenge does not introduce a sixth construct.

## Core question

> Can you put it all together?

## Player progression

The Academy has taught the player to notice, understand, choose, adapt and move. The Final asks the player to combine those abilities naturally without treating them as five separate mini-games.

## Gameplay structure

### Phase 1 — Build

Accessible scenarios that reinforce confidence and integrated thinking.

### Phase 2 — Pressure

Increased tempo, uncertainty and tactical complexity.

### Phase 3 — Match

Representative sequences requiring scanning, anticipation, decision-making, adaptation and intelligent movement within the same passage of play.

## Scoring

The Final validates and integrates the existing profile. It does not create a separate Match Challenge construct. Overall FIQ is informed by evidence across the five missions, with the Final providing the strongest evidence of integration.

## Match Mentality

Longer sequences provide the richest observation of persistence, recovery, learning, composure and consistency.

## Graduation output

- Football IQ profile;
- Match Mentality profile;
- strengths;
- development priorities;
- recommended training pathway.

## Coaching message

> Play the game. Trust your football.

## Success criterion

The Academy ends with a performance and opens the next development cycle rather than presenting graduation as an endpoint.
