# Assessment 3 — Best Option

**Codename:** BO-01  
**Status:** Locked

**Primary FIQ:** Decision Quality  
**Secondary FIQ:** Awareness, Game Reading, Use of Space

## Purpose

Measure how effectively a player evaluates context and selects the highest-value football action.

## Core question

> What should you do?

## Gameplay

1. Watch a representative football sequence.
2. Pause at the key decision moment.
3. Choose an action such as pass, carry, shoot, press, cover, support or hold.
4. See the likely consequence and comparison with stronger alternatives.
5. Receive immediate coaching feedback.

## Scoring

Options are weighted on a continuum: excellent, good, acceptable, poor or high-risk. Football is not reduced to arbitrary binary answers where multiple viable options exist.

Measures include decision quality, tactical reasoning, risk calibration, decision efficiency and consistency.

## Match Mentality

Composure, recovery, persistence, learning and consistency under increasing pressure.

## Transfer target

Better pass selection, possession management, attacking efficiency, defensive choices and fewer forced errors.

## Coaching message

> Choose the smartest play.

## Success criterion

The player understands that the best decision depends on the situation rather than a fixed rule.
