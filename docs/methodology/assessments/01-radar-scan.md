# Assessment 1 — Radar Scan

**Codename:** RS-01  
**Status:** Locked

**Primary FIQ:** Awareness  
**Secondary FIQ:** Game Reading, Decision Quality

## Purpose

Measure how effectively a player gathers and prioritises useful information from a representative football scene.

## Core question

> What do you notice?

## Gameplay

1. Observe a short football scene.
2. The scene disappears or freezes.
3. Answer a representative question about teammates, opponents, pressure, passing lanes or open space.
4. Receive immediate coaching feedback.
5. Repeat with increasing complexity.

## Measures

- awareness accuracy;
- cue prioritisation;
- information efficiency;
- decision readiness;
- response time, secondary to quality.

## Match Mentality

Persistence, recovery, learning, composure and consistency are inferred across attempts.

## Transfer target

Earlier scanning, stronger pressure recognition, improved support play and better preparation before receiving the ball.

## Coaching message

> See more. Understand sooner.

## Success criterion

The player leaves understanding that good players gather useful information before acting.
