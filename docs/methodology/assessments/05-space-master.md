# Assessment 5 — Space Master

**Codename:** SM-01  
**Status:** Locked

**Primary FIQ:** Use of Space  
**Secondary FIQ:** Awareness, Game Reading, Decision Quality

## Purpose

Measure how effectively a player recognises, creates, occupies and protects useful space before and during play.

## Core question

> Where should you be?

## Gameplay

1. Watch a representative sequence.
2. Pause before the next phase.
3. Tap the best movement or position on the pitch.
4. Resume play to show how that movement affects teammates, opponents and available options.
5. Receive immediate coaching feedback.

Reward movement that benefits the team, including movement that creates space for another player.

## Measures

- spatial awareness;
- positional intelligence;
- movement timing;
- support quality;
- defensive positioning.

## Match Mentality

Patience, persistence, learning, composure and consistency.

## Transfer target

Better support play, availability to receive, attacking runs, defensive shape and team connectivity.

## Coaching message

> Find the space. Create the advantage.

## Success criterion

The player understands that where they move can be as important as what they do with the ball.
