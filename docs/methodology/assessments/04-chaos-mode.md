# Assessment 4 — Chaos Mode

**Codename:** CM-01  
**Status:** Locked

**Primary FIQ:** Adaptability  
**Secondary FIQ:** Decision Quality, Game Reading, Awareness

## Purpose

Measure how effectively a player recognises meaningful change, abandons an outdated plan and selects a stronger alternative.

## Core question

> What changes your decision?

## Gameplay

1. Watch a realistic sequence and begin forming a plan.
2. A genuine football change occurs: a lane closes, runner changes direction, defender recovers, pressure arrives or possession changes.
3. Select the best new action.
4. See the consequence.
5. Receive brief coaching feedback.

Changes must be representative rather than artificial gotcha moments.

## Measures

- adaptation accuracy;
- plan switching;
- cognitive flexibility;
- response efficiency;
- decision stability.

## Match Mentality

Recovery and composure are especially visible, alongside persistence, learning and consistency.

## Transfer target

Better transition responses, faster tactical adjustment, fewer forced mistakes and improved recovery after turnovers.

## Coaching message

> Great players do not panic. They adapt.

## Success criterion

The player leaves believing: when football changes, I can change with it.
