# PitchIQ Sports Science Lab

## Purpose
The Sports Science Lab protects the evidence base behind PitchIQ.

## Focus Areas
- Psychology
- Growth mindset
- Wise interventions
- Cognitive training
- Biofeedback
- Apple Watch
- LiDAR
- IMU
- Research backlog

PitchIQ should reinforce scanning, action, recovery, persistence, and adaptive pressure without shame-based feedback.

## MVP Rule
Sports science features must not expand MVP scope unless approved by Executive Board.
